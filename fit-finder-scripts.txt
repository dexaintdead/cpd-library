     reads like it was written for that specific person.

  •  Don't follow up more than twice. If two nudges don't land, move
     on — the Library and Fit Finder are working for you in the
     background; someone else will convert.

  •  Every share you send through the Library auto-logs to your
     referral tracker. You'll see it in the Earnings tool.

  •  The 40% is recurring. One conversion on the $500/mo Business Plus
     tier = $200/mo, forever, as long as they stay. Worth the 30
     seconds it took to paste the link.
